Precautions

Pregnancy (use in the first trimester is not recommended; during the rest of pregnancy, use only if drug therapy is
essential).

SKILLED TASKS. May impair ability to drive or operate machinery.
May cause somnolence. Take caution while driving or
performing other tasks which require alertness.

Adverse Drug Reactions

Rare: Diarrhea, dizziness, GI discomfort, nausea, skin rash, somnolence, urticaria

Drug Interactions

Avoid concomitant use with:
Incompatible with the following products:
Penicillins, Phenobarbital, Salicylates, Tetracyclines

Increases risk of adverse or toxic effects of Butamirate:
Expectorants (bronchospasm; airways infection)

Mechanism of Action

A centrally-acting antitussive, which is neither chemically nor pharmacologically related to the opioids, intended as a
cough suppressant.
