Dosage

ORAL › 250 mg tablet (Rx)

Fascioliasis, by mouth, ADULT and ADOLESCENT, 10 mg/kg as a single dose, may be repeated after 2 weeks or 6
months if necessary; CHILD, 10 mg/kg as a single dose. Fascioliasis, severe, by mouth, ADULT and ADOLESCENT,
20 mg/kg in 2 divided doses 12 hours apart. Paragonimiasis, by mouth, ADULT and ADOLESCENT, 5 mg/kg once
daily for 3 days; or 10 mg/kg as a single dose; CHILD, 10 mg/kg as a single dose; or 20 mg/kg in 2 divided doses for 1
day.

Administration

ORAL › 250 mg tablet (Rx)

To be taken with fatty food to increase absorption. MISSED DOSE. Take missed dose as soon as remembered. Do
NOT take if near time for next dose. Do NOT take 2 doses at once.

Indications

For the treatment of paragonimiasis; primary or alternate agent to praziquantel; for implementation of the DPCB –
Food and Waterborne Disease Program in identified endemic areas in the Philippines

Contraindications

Breastfeeding

Precautions

Pregnancy (studies in humans have not been conducted); lactation (excreted in breast milk).

Adverse Drug Reactions

Common and Less Common: Transient self-limiting biliary obstruction, gastrointestinal disturbance, abdominal
cramps, diarrhea

Drug Interactions

No information found

Mechanism of Action

A benzimidazole compound that is active against immature and adult Fasciola parasites.
