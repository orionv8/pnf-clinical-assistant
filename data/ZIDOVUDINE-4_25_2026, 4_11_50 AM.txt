Dosage

Administration

Dose Adjustment

Renal Impairment:
CrCl <15mL/minute: 100mg every 6-8 hours, alternate dosing: 100mg 3 times a day or 300 mg once a day; End-Stage
Renal Disease on Intermittent Hemodialysis (admin dose after dialysis on dialysis days): 100mg every 6-8 hours,
alternate dosing: 100mg 3 times a day or 300mg/day. Peritoneal Dialysis: 100mg every 6-8 hours.

Indications

Treatment of HIV-1 infection in combination with at least two other antiretroviral agents.

Contraindications

Neutrophil count <750/mm3 or hemoglobin <7.5g/dL (4.65mmol/L).

Precautions

WARNING: matologic toxicity; Lactic acidosis/severe hepatomegaly.

Monitor patients for: Fat Redistribution; Hematologic Toxicity, Immune Reconstitution Syndrome), Lactic Acidosis,
Severe Hepatomegaly with Steatosis, Myopathy.

Adverse Drug Reactions

Common: Headache, malaise, nausea, anorexia, vomiting.

Less common: Constipation, anemia, muscle weakness.

Rare: Cardiomyopathy, rash, body fat redistribution, abdominal pain, hepatitis, hearing loss.

Drug Interactions

Monitor closely with:
Increases risk of adverse or toxic effects of Zidovudine:
Aciclovir, Valaciclovir, Divalproex, Fluconazole,
Interferons, Protease inhibitors, Rifamycin
Avoid concomitant use with:
Reduces therapeutic effects of Stavudine
Increases risk of adverse or toxic effects of the following drugs:
Ribavirin, Ganciclovir, Valganciclovir, Doxorubicin,
Clarithromycin
