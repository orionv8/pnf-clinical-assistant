Dosage

ORAL › tablet (equivalent to 500 mg elemental calcium)(as lactate) (OTC)

Hypocalcemia, by mouth, ADULT, 15.4–30 g daily divided every 8 hours; CHILD, 345–500 mg/kg daily in divided doses
every 6–8 hours (maximum dose, 9 g daily); INFANT, 400–500 mg/kg daily in divided doses every 4– 6 hours.
Nutritional supplementation, by mouth, ADULT, 1000 mg daily; ADOLESCENT and CHILD =9 years, 1300 mg daily;
CHILD 4–8 years, 800 mg daily; CHILD 1–3 years, 500 mg daily; INFANT 6–12 months, 270 mg daily; INFANT <6 months
and NEONATE, 210 mg daily.

Administration

ORAL › tablet (equivalent to 500 mg elemental calcium)(as lactate) (OTC)

Administer 1–1.5 hours after meals or with a demulcent (e.g., milk). Follow each dose with appropriate fluid intake.
Separate administration with other medicines to prevent interactions.

Dose Adjustment

N/A

Indications

Management of hypocalcemia; nutritional supplementation

Mechanism of Action

An organic Calcium salt used to prevent or treat negative calcium balance. It also helps to prevent or reduce the rate
of bone loss, moderate nerve and muscle performance and allow normal cardiac function.
