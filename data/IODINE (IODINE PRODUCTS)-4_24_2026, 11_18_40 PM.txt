Contraindications

Hypersensitivity to iodine or any component in the formulation

Precautions

Stains skin and clothing

Adverse Drug Reactions

Common: Skin irritation

Drug Interactions

For external use only. Apply topically to the affected areas as necessary.

Do NOT cover iodine-treated wounds to avoid irritation. Do NOT apply over large areas of the body.

Avoid contact with eyes. If contact occurs, flush with large amounts of water while lifting upper and lower lids.

Mechanism of Action

A nonmetallic element with germicidal activity.
