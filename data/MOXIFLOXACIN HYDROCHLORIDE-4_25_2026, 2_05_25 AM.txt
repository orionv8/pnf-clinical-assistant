Contraindications

Hypersensitivity to moxifloxacin, other quinolones, or to any component of the formulation.

Precautions

May cause severe hypersensitivity reactions including
anaphylaxis, angioedema, dermatologic reactions, and
superinfection.

Adverse Drug Reactions

Less Common:
Eye irritation, conjunctivitis, decreased
visual acuity, eye discomfort, eye pain, eye pruritus,
lacrimation, ocular hyperemia, subconjunctival
hemorrhage, and xerophthalmia.

Drug Interactions

None known.

Mechanism of Action

Moxifloxacin is a DNA gyrase inhibitor, and also inhibits topoisomerase IV. DNA gyrase is required for DNA replication
and transcription, DNA repair, recombination, and transposition; inhibition is bactericidal.
