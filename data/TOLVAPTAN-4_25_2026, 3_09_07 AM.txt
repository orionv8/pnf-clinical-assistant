Indications

Hyponatremia - Tolvaptan is indicated for the treatment of patients with clinically significant hypervolemic and
euvolemic hyponatremia (serum sodium < 125 mEq/L or less marked hyponatremia that is symptomatic and has
resisted correction with fluid restriction), including those with heart failure, and Syndrome of Inappropriate
Antidiuretic Hormone (SIADH).
