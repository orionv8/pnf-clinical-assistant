Administration

Indications

Active immunization against tetanus when combination antigen preparations are not indicated; tetanus prophylaxis
in wound management

Contraindications

Hypersensitivity to tetanus toxoid or any component of the formulation

Adverse Drug Reactions

Common:
Hypotension, brachial neuritis, fever, pain, malaise, nausea, injection site reactions (edema, induration, rash,
redness, urticaria, warmth), arthralgia, Guillain-Barré syndrome, Arthrus-type hypersensitivity
reaction, anaphylactic reaction

Mechanism of Action

A sterile solution of tetanus toxoid in isotonic sodium chloride solution, which is used as a booster injection against
tetanus.
