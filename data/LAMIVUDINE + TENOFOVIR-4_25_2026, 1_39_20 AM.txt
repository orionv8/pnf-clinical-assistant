Dosage

ORAL › 300 mg + 300 mg tablet (Rx)

Treatment of HIV-1 infection, by mouth, ADULT, one tablet taken once daily

Administration

ORAL › 300 mg + 300 mg tablet (Rx)

Tablet should be swallowed WHOLE with water, taken on an empty stomach since food may increase Efavirenz
exposure and may lead to an increase in the frequency of adverse reactions. Not recommended for administration
to CHILDREN.

Dose Adjustment

Renal Impairment:
Not recommended for patients with moderate or severe renal impairment, may require dose interval adjustment of
lamivudine and tenofovir that cannot be achieved with combination tablet.

Indications

First-line treatment for HIV infection which consists of at least 3 agents

Contraindications

Hypersensitivity to tenofovir, lamivudine or any excipient in the formulation

Precautions

WARNING: Chronic hepatitis B and lactic acidosis or hepatomegaly may occur.
Should not be administered concomitantly with other Cytidine analogues such as Emtricitabine.
Refer to individual drug monographs of lamivudine, and tenofovir.
