Composition: Concentration 5–10% Protein 5–10 g; including taurine Calories 20-40 kcal Electrolytes none

Dosage

IV INFUSION › 100 mL (Rx)

NOTE: Individualize dose based on patient’s needs. For parenteral nutrition, by IV infusion, INFANT, administer as
required, not exceeding maximum dose, as follows: Maximum daily dosage 1st year of living 1.5 – 2.5 g/kg 2nd–5th
year of living 1.5 g/kg 6th–14th year of living 1 g/kg

Administration

IV INFUSION › 100 mL (Rx)

For IV infusion. Keep out of reach of children. NOTE: Too rapid infusion may result in renal losses causing amino
acid imbalance. See General Information on Amino Acid Solutions listed above for other information.

Dose Adjustment

Renal and Hepatic Impairment:
Individualize doses.

Indications

For partial parenteral nutrition in premature
infants, babies, and small children

Contraindications

Disturbances in amino acid metabolism, acidosis, hyperhydration, hypokalemia

Precautions

Hyponatremia.

Mechanism of Action

A mixture of amino acids, carbohydrates, and fat emulsions that may serve as total parenteral nutrition.

Pregnancy & Remarks

PREGNANCY CATEGORY

Not applicable
