Indications

Nutritional support in children to provide energy
and nutrients to support optimal growth, and neuromotor
development; inability to take enough food; maldigestion
and malabsorption; increased nutritional requirements
(e.g., burns, trauma, cystic fibrosis, congenital heart
disease); altered metabolism; primary disease
management (e.g., chronic diarrhea, Crohn’s disease)

NOTE: To be used on pediatric populations with growth
impairment but sufficiently preserved GI function:
Child <2 years with growth failure >1 month
Child >2 years with growth failure >3 months
Change on centile charts >2 growth channels
Triceps skinfolds <5th percentile per age

Mechanism of Action

Contains protein, fat, and carbohydrates, which require
normal or near normal digestive and absorptive capacity and are complete with respect to vitamin, minerals, and
trace elements at a specified volume.
