Dosage

IT › 45 mg/mL (54 mg/1.2 mL) lyophilized powder for suspension (intratracheal admistration) (Rx)

Respiratory distress syndrome, by intratracheal injection, NEONATE, 4 mL per kg birth weight. Four doses can be
administered in the first 48 hours of life. Doses should be given no more frequently than every 6 hours.

Administration

IT › 45 mg/mL (54 mg/1.2 mL) lyophilized powder for suspension (intratracheal admistration) (Rx)

For intratracheal administration only.

Dose Adjustment

Respiratory distress syndrome, by intratracheal injection, NEONATE, 4 mL per kg birth weight. Four doses can be
administered in the first 48 hours of life. Doses should be given no more frequently than every 6 hours.

Indications

Treatment of respiratory distress syndrome in premature neonates.

Precautions

May cause transient rales and moist breath sounds after administration;
Increased probability of post-treatment nosocomial sepsis;
Use in infant less than 600 g or more than 1750 g birth
weight has not been evaluated.

Adverse Drug Reactions

Common:
Bradycardia and oxygen desaturation.

Rare: Endotracheal tube reflux, pallor, vasoconstriction, hypotension, endotracheal tube blockage, hypertension,
hypocarbia, hypercarba, and apnea.

Drug Interactions

No information provided in the manufacturer’s labeling.

Mechanism of Action

A sterile, non-pyrogenic pulmonary surfactant intended for intratracheal use only.
