Administration

Indications

For water purification for use in cleaning hospital facilities, washing of hands, gloves, and other daily use articles,
and sterilizing of items used in hospitals; disinfection of water in the prevention of water-borne diseases

Precautions

WARNING: Not for consumption. Do NOT ingest tablet.

Ensure that use of tablets, especially for large volume waters, is conducted in a well-ventilated area.

Adverse Drug Reactions

Rare: Cough, sore throat, redness, skin burns, pain

Mechanism of Action

The sodium salt of a chlorinated hydroxytriazine used as a source of free available chlorine, in the form of
hypochlorous acid, for the disinfection of water.
