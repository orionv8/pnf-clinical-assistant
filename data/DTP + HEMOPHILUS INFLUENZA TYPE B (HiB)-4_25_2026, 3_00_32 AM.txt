DTP + HEMOPHILUS INFLUENZA TYPE B (HiB)

Drug Classification

ATC CODE

J07AG52; J07AJ51

ANATOMICAL

Anti-Infectives for systemic use

THERAPEUTIC

Vaccines

PHARMACOLOGICAL

Bacterial Vaccines

Technical Specifications PNF Primary Care Reserved AMs

IM › SC › 0.5 mL DTP diluent in pre-filled syringe + freeze-dried 10 micrograms Hib vial (Rx)

Dosage

Administration

Indications

Active immunization against diphtheria, tetanus, pertussis, and invasive disease caused by Haemophilus influenzae
type b (Hib)

Contraindications

Hypersensitivity to the any component of the formulation; severe acute neurological illness within 7 days of
pertussis vaccination; thrombocytopenia or any coagulation disorders

Mechanism of Action

A combination product containing adsorbed diphtheria, tetanus, whole cell pertussis, and Haemophilus influenzae
type b vaccines.
