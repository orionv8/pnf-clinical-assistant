Dosage

RESPIR › 2.5 mg/mL (as sulfate), 2 mL (unit dose) (Rx)

Severe bronchospasm, by inhalation of nebulized solution, as 1% solution, ADULT, 2.5–10 mg 2 to 4 times daily;
CHILD ≥25 kg: 5 mg 2–4 times daily

Administration

RESPIR › 2.5 mg/mL (as sulfate), 2 mL (unit dose) (Rx)

Keep out of reach of children and pets. Consult with pharmacist on discarding unused drugs. See Terbutaline
under Other Gynecologicals –Sympathomimetics, Labor Repressants in Chapter 5: Genito Urinary System and Sex
Hormones for other Information.

Indications

Bronchodilator in reversible airway obstruction and bronchial asthma

Contraindications

Acute or chronic use in the prevention, treatment, or management of preterm labor
