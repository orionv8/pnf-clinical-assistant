CHEMICAL CLASS

Technical Specifications PNF Primary Care Reserved AMs

IM › 10 mcg/0.5 mL in 1 Dose vial + 0.5 mL diluent with tetanus protein (IM) (Rx)

IM › 10 mcg/0.5 mL Vial + 0.9% sodium chloride with diptheria CRM 197 protein (IM) (Rx)

IM › 0.5 mL Vial + Meningococcal protein (IM) (Rx)

Dosage

Administration

IM › 10 mcg/0.5 mL in 1 Dose vial + 0.5 mL diluent with tetanus protein (IM) (Rx)

For IM administration into the anterolateral thigh or deltoid muscle. Do NOT administer into buttocks due to
potential risk of injury to sciatic nerve. Do NOT inject by IV, ID, or SC. Shake well prior to use. Do NOT mix with other
vaccines or injections. Use different needles and syringes for each injection. Administer while seated or lying
down to prevent syncope related injuries.

Indications

Active immunization for the prevention of invasive disease caused by Haemophilus influenzae type b (Hib)
