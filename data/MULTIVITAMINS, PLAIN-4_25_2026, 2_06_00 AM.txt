Dosage

ORAL › 1 mL drops (OTC)

Prevention or treatment of vitamin deficiencies: by mouth, ADULT, 1 tablet or capsule daily.

Administration

ORAL › 1 mL drops (OTC)

May be taken with or without food. It may be taken with meals for better absorption or if GI discomfort occurs.

Dose Adjustment

N/A

Indications

Dietary supplementation, management of vitamin deficiencies

Contraindications

Known hypersensitivity to any component of the preparations

Precautions

Avoid taking similar vitamin products

NOTE: Not all products can be used in children of all age groups. Consult specific product labeling before use. Do
NOT exceed recommended doses.

Adverse Drug Reactions

No known ADRs found

Drug Interactions

Decreases serum concentration of certain components of Multivitamins: Food, e.g., eggs, milk (inhibits the
absorption of iron)

Mechanism of Action

A dietary supplement containing essential multivitamins and minerals that are needed for good health, growth, and
development.
