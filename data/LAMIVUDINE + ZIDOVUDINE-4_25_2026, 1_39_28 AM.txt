Precautions

WARNING:
Zidovudine is associated with hematologic toxicity including neutropenia and severe anemia.
Lactic acidosis and severe hepatomegaly with steatosis.
Prolonged use of Zidovudine has been associated with symptomatic myopathy and myositis; Monitor closely for
chronic hepatitis B for several months following discontinuation of therapy in patients co-infected with HBV and HIV.

Refer to individual drug monographs of Lamivudine and Zidovudine.
