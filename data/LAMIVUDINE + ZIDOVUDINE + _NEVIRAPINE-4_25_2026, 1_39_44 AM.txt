Contraindications

Severe Anemia (< 7.5 g/dL or 4.65 mmol/L); Neutropenia (< 0.75 x 109/L); Permanent liver disease or marked changes
in liver function; History of liver inflammation; History of skin rash or liver injury while on treatment with nevirapine-
containing products;

Medication therapy with rifampicin; Children <25kg.

Precautions

Monitor patients for: liver damage, blood disorders (anemia and neutropenia), hypersensitivity reactions, lactic
acidosis, immune reactivation syndrome, osteonecrosis; It is recommended that separate preparations of
Lamivudine, Zidovudine and Nevirapine be administered when any dosage adjustment is necessary.
