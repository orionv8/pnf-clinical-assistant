Indications

Second line agent for luminal infection; amebicide; intestinal amoebiasis; for hepatic abscess secondary to E.
histolytica

Precautions

Pregnancy and lactation (use with caution).

Adverse Drug Reactions

Common: Flatulence

Less Common: Vomiting, pruritus, urticaria, anorexia

Drug Interactions

No information found

Mechanism of Action

A dichloroacetamide-derived luminal amoebicide acting mainly in the bowel lumen. It is given alone in asymptomatic
cyst passers. For patients with active amoebic infections, it can be administered with a 5- nitroimidazole e.g.
metronidazole or tinidazole.

Pregnancy & Remarks

PREGNANCY CATEGORY

Not classified
