Dosage

ORO › 1% antiseptic solution, 60 mL bottle (OTC)

Oral hygiene, by mouth or throat, ADULT and CHILD >6 years, rinse mouth with 10 mL 0.1% solution, undiluted or
diluted with an equal amount of warm water for 30 seconds 4 times daily for 14 days. Oral candidiasis, by mouth or
throat, ADULT, rinse with 1% solution.

Administration

ORO › 1% antiseptic solution, 60 mL bottle (OTC)

Do NOT ingest. Ensure to spit out the solution after thorough rinsing. May color teeth and mucous membranes.

Indications

oral hygiene; oral candidiasis

Contraindications

prolonged use in patients with thyroid disorders or on lithium therapy; premature neonates or neonates weighing
<1.5 kg

Precautions

Avoid contact with eyes; lactation; neonates.

Adverse Drug Reactions

Rare: Local irritation, sensitivity

Drug Interactions

Monitor closely with:
Incompatible with the following products:
Bupivacaine (Liposomal) [allow the site to dry completely
before administering bupivacaine], Fibrin Sealant
(Human) [thoroughly rinse and dry prior to application of
sealant]

Avoid concomitant use with:
Incompatible with the following products:
Benzalkonium Chloride, Collagenase

TEST INTERACTIONS. May interfere with thyroid function test
results and interpretation if used for prolonged periods.

Mechanism of Action

An iodophore with a powerful broad-spectrum germicidal activity against a wide range of bacteria, viruses, fungi,
protozoa and spores.
