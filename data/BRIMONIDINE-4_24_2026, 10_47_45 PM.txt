For lowering of intraocular pressure, into the eye, ADULT, instill 1 drop into the affected eye(s) every 8 hours.

Administration

OPHTHALM › 0.15% ophthalmic solution (as tartrate), 5 mL bottle (Rx)

For ophthalmic use only. NOTE: May be used concomitantly with other topical ophthalmic drug products to lower
intraocular pressure. If more than one topical ophthalmic product is being used, administer at least 5 minutes
apart.

For the lowering of intraocular pressure in open-angle glaucoma or ocular hypertension

Drug Interactions

Monitor closely with:
Enhances therapeutic effect of Brimonidine:
CNS Depressants e.g., Alcohol (additive or potentiation effect)

Increases risk of adverse or toxic effects of Brimonidine:
MAO Inhibitors e.g., Sertraline (systemic side effects)

Avoid concomitant use with:
Enhances therapeutic effect of Alpha2 Agonists e.g., Methyldopa (reduced blood pressure)

Decreases metabolism of Brimonidine:
Tricyclic Antidepressants e.g., Amitriptyline (increased uptake of circulating amines)

Mechanism of Action

A relatively selective alpha2 adrenergic agonist for ophthalmic use. It acts by reducing aqueous humor and
increasing uveoscleral outflow.
