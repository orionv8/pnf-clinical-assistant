Precautions

WARNING: Black box warning of chronic hepatitis B and lactic acidosis or hepatomegaly.
Use with caution in patients with risk factors for liver disease (risk may be increased with female sex, obesity,
pregnancy or prolonged exposure) and suspend treatment in any patient who develops clinical or laboratory findings
suggestive of lactic acidosis or hepatotoxicity.

Adverse Drug Reactions

Common: Insomnia, abdominal pain, muscle weakness.
Less Common: Chest pain, fatigue, hyperglycemia.
Rare: Neutropenia, diaphoresis.

Drug Interactions

Avoid concomitant use with:
Reduces therapeutic effect of Didanosine
