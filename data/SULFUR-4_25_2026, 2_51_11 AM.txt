Dosage

TOPIC › 10% cream / ointment, 15 g tube (OTC)

Scabies, by topical application, ADULT, apply a thin layer uniformly and massage gently into all skin surfaces from
the neck to the toes (including the soles), leave on for 24 hours before washing, repeat application for 2–3
successive days, 30 g is sufficient for each application; CHILD, use a smaller proportion of adult dose.

Administration

TOPIC › 10% cream / ointment, 15 g tube (OTC)

For external use only. Cleanse skin thoroughly. Cover the entire affected area with a thin layer and leave on for 24
hours. Thoroughly remove with water. Avoid contact with eyes, mouth and other mucous membranes. Do NOT use
on broken skin Do NOT use concurrently with other topical acne medications unless otherwise directed by
physician.

Indications

Treatment of scabies

Contraindications

Children <2 years

Precautions

May stain the skin black and give off a foul smell when applied concomitantly with mercurial compounds.

Adverse Drug Reactions

Skin irritation, dermatitis

Drug Interactions

Enhances therapeutic effect of Sulfur: Resorcinol

Mechanism of Action

A keratolytic, mild antiseptic, mild antifungal, and parasiticide.

Pregnancy & Remarks

PREGNANCY CATEGORY

Not classified
