Dosage

ORAL › 400 micrograms folic acid + 60 mg elemental iron per tablet / capsule / FC tablet (Rx)

ORAL Prevention of iron and folate deficiencies in pregnancy: by mouth, ADULT, 100 mg elemental iron + 350 to
400 micrograms folic acid daily throughout pregnancy.

Administration

ORAL › 400 micrograms folic acid + 60 mg elemental iron per tablet / capsule / FC tablet (Rx)

To be taken on an empty stomach, at least 1 hour before or 2 hours after meal. Avoid taking antacids or antibiotics
within 2 hours before or after taking it. See Ferrous Salt + Folic Acid under Mineral Supplements in Chapter 1:
Alimentary Tract and Metabolism for other information.

Indications

Prevention of iron and folic acid deficiencies, especially in pregnancy.

Mechanism of Action

A two-component, nutritional supplement of iron and folic acid given during pregnancy.
